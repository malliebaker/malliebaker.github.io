My Personal Mission Statement

As a professional school counselor, I believe that all students have the ability to succeed academically, personally, and within the career of their choosing. By collaborating with school stakeholders, I can help students become well-rounded and successful individuals. Children should come first. Each student should be treated both as an individual and as part of the larger school community. With proper support and encouragement, all students are capable of learning.
